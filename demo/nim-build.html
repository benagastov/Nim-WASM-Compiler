How it works (3 stages, all in the browser):
  1. nim compiler (wasm) — compiles your Nim to C (gcc step fails by design; the .c files are what we need).
  2. clang.js (wasm) — for each .c file, runs clang.wasm to produce a .o. Then lld.wasm links all .o files into a single wasm and runs it.
Payload: ~70 MB total, lazy-loaded.
